Every sound, every style

Genres

Dive deep into the music that moves you. From chart-topping pop to underground rap, explore every corner of the musical world.

Pop 🎤

Catchy melodies, polished production, and mass appeal define pop music. From the British Invasion to today's streaming era, pop has always reflected the cultural moment.

Key Artists

Taylor Swift Sabrina Carpenter Dua Lipa
Rap 🎧

Born in the Bronx in the 1970s, hip-hop and rap have become the world's most dominant genre, blending lyrical storytelling with beats, samples, and cultural commentary.

Key Artists

Kendrick Lamar Drake Tyler the Creator
Rock 🎸

Guitar-driven and often rebellious, rock spans classic rock, punk, metal, indie, and beyond. A genre that never truly goes away — it just evolves.

Key Artists

The Beatles Nirvana Arctic Monkeys
R&B 🎹

Rooted in soul and gospel, R&B (Rhythm and Blues) is defined by smooth vocals, emotional depth, and groove-heavy production. It's the sound of vulnerability.

Key Artists

SZA Frank Ocean The Weeknd
EDM 🎛️

Electronic Dance Music takes you from intimate clubs to massive festival stages. House, techno, dubstep, trance — it's the pulse of the night.

Key Artists

Calvin Harris Skrillex Daft Punk
Jazz 🎷

America's greatest art form, jazz is built on improvisation, complexity, and expression. From New Orleans to bebop to fusion, jazz always finds new territory.

Key Artists

Miles Davis John Coltrane Herbie Hancock
Country 🪕

Rooted in folk, blues, and Appalachian music, country tells stories of heartache, hometown pride, and the open road. Modern country blends pop and hip-hop influences.

Key Artists

Morgan Wallen Zach Bryan Dolly Parton
Classical 🎻

Spanning centuries, classical music encompasses orchestral, chamber, opera, and solo works from composers whose influence echoes through all modern music.

Key Composers

Beethoven Mozart Bach
