Short n' Sweet
Sabrina Carpenter
Pop

A flawless pop record that balances humor, heartbreak, and hooks with effortless charm. Every track earns its place, and Carpenter's voice has never sounded better. A defining pop album of the decade so far.

Stick Season (We'll All Be Here Forever)
Noah Kahan
Folk / Indie
★★★★★

The expanded edition deepens an already exceptional album. Kahan's writing is painfully honest and instantly universal — few artists can make a specific Vermont winter feel this relatable to the whole world.
